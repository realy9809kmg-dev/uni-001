Simple Recipe: Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk
  2. Heat butter in a pan
  3. Pour eggs into the pan
  4. Stir gently until cooked
  5. Add salt and serve
